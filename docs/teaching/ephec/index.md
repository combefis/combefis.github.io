# EPHEC

I am working since 2022 as a **lecturer** at the [Haute École EPHEC](https://www.ephec.be) (EPHEC), a higher education institution. My role is to teach several courses for bachelor students following further education programs, in cybersecurity and human resource management. All the courses are taught in French, but some resources are only provided in English.

## Currently taught

- [**7532.14.U32.D1 – Software security**](./softwaresecurity/), 2020–now --(3 years)--  
  --_This course, taught to students in a specialisation bachelor in cybersecurity, presents the principles of secure software development and on some advanced concepts related to software security. Several practical tools are presented to students who can practice some of them on simple case studies._--
- [**7532.11.U32.D1 – Host security**](./hostsecurity/), 2022–now --(2 years)--  
  --_This course, taught to students in a specialisation bachelor in cybersecurity, presents how to secure a host in a computer system, both on the software and hardware levels. More precisely, it explains how to harden the operating system, manage access control policies and secure network elements._--
- [**0132.03.U32.D2 – Éléments de statistique**](./statistics/), 2022–now --(2 years)--  
  --_This course, taught to students in first year of bachelor in human resource management, teaches basic statistics concepts: population and sample, variable, observation, class, frequency, parameters (median, mode and mean, standard deviation), centrality and dissymmetry measures, etc._--
