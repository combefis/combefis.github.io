Welcome

Welcome on my personal homepage!

I am currently working as a lecturer at École Centrale des Arts et Métiers (ECAM), a higher education institution which trains future engineers. More precisely, I am in the Electronics and IT Unit for which I mainly teach computer science related courses.

I got a PhD in Engineering in November 2013 from the Université catholique de Louvain (UCL). My thesis is about applying formal method techniques to the analysis of human-machine interactions. I also got a Master in Computer Science Engineering degree from the Louvain School of Engineering (EPL) and an Advanced Master in Pedagogy in Higher Education, both from the same university.

I am interested in the computer science education field, to better teach computer science and to integrate IT technologies in education. I am the President of the Computer Science and IT in Education ASBL (CSITEd), a non-profit organisation whose goal is to promote informatics, in particular to secondary schools pupils. It is under that organisation that I am one of the belgian representatives for the Bebras contest.
